who am i?!

the age old question... (and a summary)

Although the vast majority of my professional experience has been in the coffee industry, I've always been an individual driven by a thirst for knowledge. This has manifested in a few different ways, from getting my Bachelor's in English where I focused on medieval literature to teaching myself HTML and CSS so that I could build my partner a CV website.

My journey with computer programming started in 2021, when I first started digging into educational resources spurred by an initial interest in UX/UI design. I very quickly discovered, though, that my true passion lies in computer programming vs. design. There is nothing I find more satisfying than breaking down complex, real-world problems into actionable steps and translating those thoughts into code. I also find the process of refactoring, optimizing, and writing tests deeply enjoyable, and am constantly searching out ways to better my problem solving skills and my code.

Throughout my time at the Tech Elevator Java bootcamp, I fostered a love for full-stack and back-end programming. I'm looking to hone my newfound technical skills working for a company that values collaborative problem-solving and communication as a software developer.
